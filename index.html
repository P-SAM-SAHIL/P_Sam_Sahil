Research Focus

Interpretability & Trust

Moving beyond "black box" optimization to design transparent AI architectures. Investigating how internal reasoning mechanisms can be made explicit and human-verifiable, specifically through neuro-symbolic frameworks.

Neuro-Symbolic AI

Bridging the gap between the scale of neural networks and the logic of symbolic reasoning. Developing dual-path architectures (like GAT + RoBERTa) to ground contextual semantics in structured knowledge.

Multilingual NLP

Breaking language barriers in emotion detection and polarization analysis. Focusing on cross-lingual generalization to capture nuance across diverse cultural contexts and low-resource languages.

Computational Social Science

Applying rigorous NLP pipelines to large-scale social data. From geo-tagging historical newspapers to analyzing online polarization, using AI to answer fundamental questions about human behavior.
